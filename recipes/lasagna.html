Lasagna

Description

An Italian dish made up of layers of pasta, bolognese sauce, belchamel sauce and topped with cheese.

Ingredients

  • Ground beef
  • Marinara sauce
  • Lasagna sheets
  • Mozzarella cheese
  • Belchamel sauce

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 200 degrees. Spray a 8 inch baking dish with non stick cooking spray.
  2. Heat a non stick skillet over medium-high heat. Crumble in beef and cook until browned.
  3. Remove from heat and stir in marinara sauce.
  4. Layer to assemble lasagna, spread 1/3 of the marinara sauce into the bottom of the baking dish. Top with 3 lasagna sheets.
  5. Spread 1/2 belchamel sauce over the lasagna sheets. Top evenly with 1/2 of the mozzarella. Top with another 1/3 of the sauce.
  6. Add 3 more lasagna sheets then spread evenly with the remaining 1/2 of the belchamel. Pour over last 1/3 of the sauce.
  7. Top with remaining mozzarella
  8. Bake in the oven for 40-45 minutes.
  9. Let rest for 5 minutes before slicing. Serve immediately.
