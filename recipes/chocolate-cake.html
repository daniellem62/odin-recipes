Chocolate Cake

Description

Moist chocolate cake with a layer of chocolate icing in the middle and chocolate icing covering it.

Ingredients

    For the cake
  • Sugar
  • Butter
  • Eggs
  • Self-raising flour
  • Cocoa powder
  • Baking powder
  • Vanilla extract
    • For the Icing
  • Butter
  • Icing sugar
  • Cocoa Powder
  • Milk

Steps

  1. Heat oven to 190 C/170 C fan. Butter the base and sides of two 20cm round sandwich tins and line the bases with baking parchment.
  2. In a large bowl, combine all ingredients for the cake until well mixed.
  3. Divide the mixture between the prepared tins. Bake for 20 mins
  4. Leave to cool in the tin for 10 mins, then turn out onto a wire rack to cool completely.
  5. In a clean bowl, combine all ingredents for the icing.
  6. On a cake stand or large plate, sandwich the cakes together with some icing, then evenly spread the rest on the surfaces of the cake.
